Video Spot My Time

Award winning work



This is the project I have realized during the two weeks of my practical exam, in the last period as apprentice at Swisscom. Thank to this two minute video I won the first prize for the Switzerland's best practical exam in 2015 in the ICT branch. 2000 other works as candidate. (ICT Young Professional Award 2015).

The task was realizing a video that synthesize the essence of a new app launched on the market by Swisscom. My Time gives the user the opportunity to track the activity of the owned device so that he can be aware of the time he spends on his smartphone.

With this Video i wanted to illustrate what the actual problem is: while spending too much time on your device we could miss some special and intime moments with the world around us. Doing so, i highlighted one of the main features of the app: taking a break from the phone so that we can focus on the non-virtual life.

Agency Prime Logo

School assignment



"Incompatible communication" is the slogan of an imaginary company, of wich we had to build a logo/branding concept as a semester work at school.

The result after a ton of paper concepts was a company logo that is simultaneously an ambigramm: if the logotype is reflected on the vertical axe, it keeps the same aspect of the company's name "Agency Prime".
I was inspired by the Illuminati logo from the Dan Brown's book/movie series.

I found that the slogan "Incompatible communication" had a (dis)armonic and powerful link with the concept of the ambigram. This and a series of other thesis helped me to argument my job and at the end, bringing home the maximum grade for the semester!

"My Time" Concept Video

Initial concept for an App



This was one of the first concepts for the app "My Time". My Time is an app that gives the user the opportunity to track the activity on the smartphone, and helps to focus on non-virtual activities by taking breaks from using the device.

The video shows with a minimal design, what the core message of the app is. This motion-graphic video is completly inspired from the "piece of art" made by Apple at the time ("Designed by Apple in Califormia") It was used for internal and training purposes.

I'm pretty proud of this short animated piece because it was my first attempt with the tool Adobe After Effects. After seeing the video made by Apple I thought "I want to animate like this!" and after about two weeks of testing/ getting-hands-on the software I started to produce this video with a good looking result.

Comic "Quick check IVR"

A comic as info-content



This 4 Page long comic has been produced for the Training division of Swisscom, with the objective to inform 3000 other co-workers about a mutation within a tool.

The comic explains in a very short story, how the coworker is going to be influenced by the new mutation, and which are going to be the changes. A robot represent the automated voice behind the phone when the customer calls Swisscom.

The production took about 4 days. The story was decided with a team, after the first day I produced the first sketches and got the approval by the team and the next three days I worked individually on the comic (final draft, ink, colours and lettering).

Live Drawing

Workshop keynote



This was a presentation my at the time person in charge as led for a workshop; sitting on the table there is me, drawing in real-time what is said and beaming the monitor directly at the audience.

After a short introduction on the thema and live drawing what the first part of the workshop was, we collected the inputs from the audience. The last part was developing one of the elements in a concrete story, and we did that by drawing what the people suggested.

This is for me a key moment that represents a nice and inspiring period in my apprenticeship. That day was almost improvvised, we didn't had too much time to prepare us but at the and the outcome was great an most important I had fun doing that!
